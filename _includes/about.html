About Me

I am a designer, illustrator, and super cool human in Orlando, Florida. I have a B.F.A. with specialization in Graphic Design from University of Central Florida. My tools of the trade are pen and paper, ink, and software such as Photoshop, Illustrator, and Sketch. I can work in the "analog" as well, drawing by hand logos, lettering, or a finished project. I like applying my skills to projects that I think better the world. My latest interest has been learning and practicing human centered design and user experience.

I also have my own cat-themed illustration business called Purrito Press. I travel to conventions to sell my work as well as submit it to shows on occasion at Orlando Museum of Art. (I swear I am not a crazy cat-lady.) I am also a Co-Captain for Code for Orlando, a Code for America Brigade. We create apps that make Orlando the best place to live, work, or play. Speaking of Orlando, I am a super huge Orlando City fan. VAMOS, VAMOS ORLANDO!
