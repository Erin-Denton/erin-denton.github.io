About me

I am a designer, illustrator, and ux enthusiast in Orlando, Florida. After going to University of Central Florida I thought I needed to relocate to find tech but I stayed and watched it blossom here in my backyard. My tools of the trade are pen and paper, ink, and software such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Sketch App, and Invision. I like applying my skills to projects that I think better the world, and make it a joy to live in. I also have my own cat-themed illustration business called Purrito Press. I am also a Co-Captain for Code for Orlando, a Code for America Brigade. We create apps that make Orlando the best place to live, work, or play. I have 1 cat, 1 dog, and 1 husband. We like to do things together, like play Dungeons and Dragons and travel. When I am not freelancing I work as a design instructor at Skillcrush.
