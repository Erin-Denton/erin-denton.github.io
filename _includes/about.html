About me

I am a designer, illustrator, UX lady, and Educator in Orlando, Florida. I like using the right tool for the right job, whether that be Adobe CC, Sketch, Figma, Invision, or pen and paper. I like applying my skills to projects that I think better the world, and make it a joy to live in. I am a former Co-Captain for Code for Orlando (now serving in an advisory capacity), a Code for America Brigade. I have 1 cat, 1 dog, and 1 husband. We like to do things together, like play Dungeons and Dragons and travel. When I am not freelancing I work as a design instructor at Skillcrush teaching Visual, Web, and UX Design.
