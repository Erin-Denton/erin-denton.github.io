About Me

I am a designer, illustrator, and super cool human in Orlando, Florida. I have a Bachelors of the Fine Arts with specialization in Graphic Design from University of Central Florida. My skills are in Photoshop, Illustrator, Pen and Paper, and a little bit of Sketch. I can work in the "analog" as well, drawing by hand logos, lettering, or a finished project. I love working on projects that encompass concepts, ideas, or causes that are important to me personally.

Aside from being a freelance designer, I also have my own cat-themed illustration business called Purrito Press. I travel to conventions to sell my work as well as submit it to shows on occasion at Orlando Museum of Art. I also volunteer as Co-Captain of our local Code for America brigade, Code for Orlando, where I help organize and lead volunteer developers, designers, and citizens to create apps that make Orlando the best city to live, work, and play in. I am also a super huge Orlando City fan. VAMOS, VAMOS ORLANDO!
